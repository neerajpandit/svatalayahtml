Admissions offered in the academic year

  • S Vatsalya Academy offer admissions to Kindergarten Nursery to Grade 8.

Application form

  • Parents who wish to apply for admission should obtain the brochure and application form school.

Criteria for submission of application form

  • The duly completed application form must be submitted in the School Office on or before the stipulated date.
  • The following documents should accompany the form:
    • Certified copy of School Report of the previous academic year (if applicable).
    • Four passport size photographs, of the child and two photographs of parents.
    • One certified copy of Child’s Birth Certificate.
    • Copy of the T.C. (If possible).
  • Original documents of the submitted photocopies should be made available upon request for verification.

Confirmation of admission

  • On receipt of acceptance of admission, parents have to pay the fees within the stipulated date.
  • The following documents should also be submitted:
    • If for any reason the Transfer Certificate cannot be produced at the time of admission, it must be produced on or before the commencement of the academic session.
